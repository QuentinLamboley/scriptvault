# ScriptVault 0.1.0

First public prototype.

- Local, content-addressed snapshots of scripts and selected research assets.
- Logical branches, tags, history, textual comparison and safe restoration.
- Optional RStudio-oriented polling watcher and project `.Rprofile` hook.
- Reproducibility records, artifact fingerprints, integrity checks and vault garbage collection.
- No Git, GitHub, remote service, account, telemetry or network synchronization required.
